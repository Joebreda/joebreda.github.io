About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science & Engineering working with Shwetak Patel in the Ubicomp Lab at the University of Washington. I am interested in using ubiquitous sensing and data science to improve the sustainability and quality of life of urban environments and society as a whole.
Outside of academics, I like to compose experimental electronic music, ambient music, and folk music. I play guitar, piano, and synthesizer. I also like to make digital art, take photos, and bike.
My favorite colors are: cream, black, lavender, cyan, pink, mint, and brown.
