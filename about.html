About Me

I am a PhD student in the Ubicomp Lab advised by Shwetal Patel in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering. My research is at the intersection of ubiquitous computing, sustainability, urban infrastructure, and global health. My main personal interest is in electronic-indie music composition and production. I do this with my mint green and rosewood Fender Telecaster and with various synthesizers and drum machines. I also like to take pictures, explore art, and explore cool places to make said art.
