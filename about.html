About Me

I am a PhD student in the Ubicomp Lab advised by Shwetal Patel in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering. My research is at the intersection of ubiquitous computing, sustainability, urban infrastructure, and global health. My main personal interest is composing and producing electronic and indie music. I play and record guitar as well as use a small MIDI keyboard and software synthesizers to (kind of) play piano. I also like to take pictures, write my thoughts, and explore nature.
